About Me

From sculpting high-poly detail to optimizing for real-time engines — I bring characters to life with speed, style, and precision.

Pipeline Expert

With over 5 years in 3D character art, I specialize in creating game-ready assets using ZBrush, Blender, Substance Painter, and Unreal Engine. My workflow integrates AI-assisted tools to accelerate iteration without sacrificing quality.
